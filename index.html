About

I am an economist at the European Commission, currently an Evaluation Officer in DG Employment, Social Affairs & Inclusion (EMPL). My work sits at the intersection of empirical economic research and EU policymaking, spanning macroeconomics, human capital, and the long-run drivers of growth and inequality.
